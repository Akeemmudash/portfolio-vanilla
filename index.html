about me

I am Akeem Mudashiru, a highly motivated Computer Engineering student with a passion for building intuitive, high-performance web applications. My journey in technology began with a deep interest in problem-solving, which led me to specialize in frontend and backend development. Through my experiences, I have developed a strong foundation in React, TypeScript, Node.js, and modern web technologies.

Currently, I am a Frontend & Backend Intern at HNG Internship, where I build AI-powered applications, event ticketing platforms, and optimize system performance. Additionally, as a Junior Frontend Developer at PluralCode Institute, I have worked on responsive landing pages, API integrations, and performance optimizations, improving load times and user experience across various platforms.

I take pride in my ability to translate complex designs into functional and scalable applications. My expertise in React, Zustand, Tailwind CSS, and RESTful API integration allows me to develop seamless, user-friendly interfaces.
